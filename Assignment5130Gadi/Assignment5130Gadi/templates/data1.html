Michael Jordan's NBA career spanned over 15 seasons, in which he has won 6 NBA championships, 5 NBA Most Valuable Player awards and 10 scoring titles. Many people to this day consider Jordan to be the greatest basketball player of all time and one of the greatest athletes in general, and he is the face of basketball for many people. Jordan became a global icon and helped the NBA to gain popularity all over the world and not just in the USA. He is now the owner of the Charlotte Hornets, and the Jordan sports brand.
